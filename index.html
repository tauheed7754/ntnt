Effective Study Strategies

Boost your preparation with these proven techniques

1 Active Recall

Test yourself frequently instead of just re-reading. This strengthens memory and identifies weak areas. Use flashcards or practice questions regularly.

2 Spaced Repetition

Review material at increasing intervals to combat the forgetting curve. Schedule revisions for optimal retention of concepts.

3 Concept Mapping

Create visual diagrams connecting related concepts. This helps in understanding relationships between topics in Biology.

4 Timed Practice

Simulate exam conditions regularly. This improves speed, accuracy, and builds stamina for the actual test day.
